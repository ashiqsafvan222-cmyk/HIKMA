================================================
HITCO WEBSITE - DEPLOYMENT INSTRUCTIONS
================================================

FILES INCLUDED:
  - index.html    (main website file)
  - favicon.png   (browser tab icon)

OPTION 1: CLOUDFLARE PAGES (FREE - RECOMMENDED)
------------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://pages.cloudflare.com
2. Sign up for a free account
3. Click "Create a project" → "Direct Upload"
4. Drag and drop BOTH files (index.html + favicon.png)
5. Click "Deploy"
6. Your site will be live at: https://your-project.pages.dev
7. To add custom domain: Settings → Custom Domains → Add

OPTION 2: NETLIFY (FREE)
------------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://app.netlify.com
2. Sign up for a free account  
3. Drag and drop BOTH files onto the deploy area
4. Your site will be live instantly
5. To add custom domain: Domain Settings → Add custom domain

OPTION 3: VERCEL (FREE)
------------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://vercel.com
2. Sign up for a free account
3. Create new project → Upload files
4. Deploy

CUSTOM DOMAIN (OPTIONAL - ~$10-15/year):
------------------------------------------------
Buy a domain from namecheap.com or godaddy.com
(e.g. hikmatrd.com or hitco.sa)
Then connect it in your hosting provider settings.
